Join as a Member Space

Note: we are currently working on our membership system, so we're not currently accepting applications for membership. We aim to be doing so by August 2017.

Membership of the UK Hackspace Foundation is open to hackspaces operating in the UK. We also offer supporter memberships to individuals.

Being a member space entitles you to:

  • Advice and support from a group of experienced hackspace founders
  • Promotion by the Hackspace Foundation on our website and at events

We're also working on many more benefits for membership in the future, including:

  • Discounts and collective purchasing from suppliers
  • A group insurance policy with lower premiums
  • Coordinating donated and spare equipment
  • Access to financial grants and loans

Requirements

In order to be eligible for membership, a hackspace must:

  • Be a member-owned and operated non-profit organisation, based in the UK
  • Have a primary object of providing a shared community workspace for members to practice science, technology, engineering, or art
  • Have a bank account
  • Have ten or more members, and an elected board of at least three directors
  • Conduct its business by allowing each member one vote
  • Foster a tolerant and inclusive community
